myBenz - the 4th version

After a few hours of playing with the launcher in my mercedes, I made a few changes to my launcher, changes related to the layout and the app' settings.

The video player was to small, almost unnoticeable, so I decided to enlarge it. Now the video player has a rotating Mercedes Benz logo, a back button, the video player and it looks like this:

The second big change is about the app's settings.

I developed a new section called Interface in the Settings menu which alows me to change different package names of the main apps that I want to appear on the first screen.

Also, a browsable video list option that allows me to choose the path to my videos.
