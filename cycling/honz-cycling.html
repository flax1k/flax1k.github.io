Honz-cycling website

In this spring, a few weeks ago I launched the Honz cycling club website.

The Honz-cycling cycling club was created on Strava, as a club on the Strava platform, as a result of gathering cyclists from the Gurahont area in a club, to pedal and explore together the surrounding areas or even those that are far away.

I always have considered that pedaling in a group is beneficial for mental and physical health, so, together with some friends, we decided to create a cycling club.

The website contains a few articles about cycling written by Codrut Avram and me, our cycling routes with memories and photos, some workouts and something about us.

Pedaling together, we overcome any limit!

We are amateur cyclists and we ride because we love this sport.
We are not interested in KOMs, times and rankings.
We ride to feel free, to explore, to live every moment, on two wheels.
