2022 in cycling

Another year has just passed by in my cycling adventure.

I will always remember the 2022 as the year when I had a bike accident and broke my clavicle. I will always remember the pain, the dissapointment, my despair and helplessness, the loneliness feelings of those minutes after the crash. The helmet has done its job and saved my life. I am not and never will be the same after that cycling incident. Two months off the bike was a serious regress.

Seeing the good part of the year, the 2022 was the year when I took part at an important cycling race, Max Ausnit 22.

Also, it was the year when I spent tens of hours in the saddle, alone or with my friends.

My 2022 cycling year was great and brought me happiness and joy, among sorrow and pain, but this is just a sport and bad things can happen too. I wish that in the 2023 to be a stronger cyclist, to explore new routes, to climb steep climbs and enjoy every ride I'll do.
