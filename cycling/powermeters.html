Powermeter pedals

For this year of my cycling hobby, I have ordered the Assioma Favero Duo - a cycling power meter set with sensors on both pedals.

Power meters measure the mechanical power I release at the level of the pedals; all the real-time data (measured in watts) is sent to my Ultra bike computer and serves as an objective reference for my performance.

These power meter pedals are extremely accurate, very light (151.gr), compatible with Look-Keo / Shimano cleats, IAV cycling dynamics, easily transferable between bikes, bluetooth and ANT+ connectivity, auto-calibration system.
