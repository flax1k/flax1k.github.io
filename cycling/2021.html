2021 in cycling

The year of 2021 was my best and most productive year in cycling.

It was the year when I've reached in 236 rides, the 10.000 km distance, with 332 hours of pedaling and 53100m of elevation.

I made new cycling friends, I rode new beautiful and various routes with my Ultra bike.

It was the year when I ride the Padis (86km with 1100m elevation), The Codru Moma loop (200km), BRM200 (233km) and many other rides over 80km/ride.

My cycling power has been increased a lot, making me more confident in my potential and cycling endurance.

My 2021 cycling year was the greatest and fullest cycling year I ever had, bringing me a lot of happiness and joy. I just pray to God to be healthy and able to continue this pedaling sport in 2022, to be able to push my limits, to be stronger and faster.
