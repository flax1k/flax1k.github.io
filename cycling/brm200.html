BRM 200 by RandoROM Arad

In 11th Sep 2021 I took part to my first organized cycling event, a great and unique experience.

On September 11, 2021, was celebrated the 100th anniversary of the first 200 km endurance patent in the world. As a result, RandoROM Arad has organized a centenary ride event: 1921-2021 BRM 200 CENTENAR Arad.

The route was established with the departure from Arad, Ceala forest, the first checkpoint at Pancota, the second checkpoint and a snack at Moneasa, the third checkpoint at Pancota (on return) and the arrival at the same point as at departure, via Curtici.

For me, it was a lifetime event to remember, a super great ride for a long distance with new cycling experiences, very well organized, warm & funny people and new cycling lovers friends. One of the most beautiful day of my life.

With this event, I felt part of a big family with the same passion for cycling.
