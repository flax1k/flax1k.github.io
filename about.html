About Me

Hi!
I am Flavius Cristiu, aka flaX1k, just an unknown cyber developer and in my spare time a cyclist, originally from a village named Gurahont, Arad county in Romania.
I was educated at "Avram Iancu" highschool from Brad and Politehnica University from Timisoara, but I'm living in Gurahont village, where I found my peace and life's meaning.
In my spare time, I mainly ride my bikes or squeeze my brains with programming stuff. For me, cycling is about feeling free, enjoying views, gasping on steep climbs, managing disappointments and maintaining consistency. Programming is just another hobby of mine and I code mostly in java, javascript, python and HTML. I also have some passions for smarthomes, smart things, AI, new technologies or my Bomber car Mercedes.

I'm wasting my talents being an employee at HT Est Division from Gurahont, Romania, like a Porche of 500HP running on a highway at 80km/h only.

You can find me on Instagram, Twitter, Github or my Strava account (for cycling enthusiasts).
